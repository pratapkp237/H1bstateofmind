About Me

I'm Pratap Krishnan, a data scientist by profession with experience at companies like 84.51 and Instacart. I've lived and worked in cities across the U.S., Canada, and India—including San Francisco, Seattle, Toronto, Cincinnati, New Delhi, and Bangalore.

Having traveled extensively through Asia, Latin America, and North America, I bring a global lens to everything I do. My mission is to support and empower those on the immigrant journey—helping them navigate transitions, build meaningful connections, and thrive in new environments.
